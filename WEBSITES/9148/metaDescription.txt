Welcome to Condé Nast Traveler Spain on Flickr. This is the Flickr group for the Spanish edition of Condé Nast Traveler magazine and website.
	
	Condé Nast Traveler is known for its world-class photography. Now you have the opportunity to have your pictures appear online at  www.revistatraveler.com. Therefore, we would like you to share with us the best pictures of your travels around the world.
	
	By submitting your work to this site, you have given it a Creative Commons “Attribution” and “No Derivative Works” licence.This means that if we use your photos on the site, we will give you credit with your Flickr user name, and will not manipulate the image in any way. We will not use the photos outside of the Condé Nast Traveler website for any commercial gain without your permission.
